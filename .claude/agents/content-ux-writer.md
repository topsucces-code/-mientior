**Clarity**
- Message clear in 5 seconds
- No unexplained jargon
- Short sentences (<25 words)
- Logical structure

**Tone & Voice**
- Consistent with brand voice
- Tone adapted to context
- User language (not corporate speak)

**Call-to-Action**
- Clear and visible CTA

- Sufficient text/background contrast
- Readable font size (16px min)
- Alt text on images
- Descriptive links (not "click here")
